This "MuscleForceCalculator" computes the maximal isometric muscle forces of a subject based on its height and mass. The subject's total muscle volume is scaled using Handsfield's method (see reference below).

======================
Handsfield, G. G.; Meyer, C. H.; Hart, J. M.; Abel, M. F. & Blemker, S. S.
Relationships of 35 lower limb muscles to height and body mass quantified using MRI
Journal of Biomechanics, Elsevier BV, 2014, 47, 631-638
